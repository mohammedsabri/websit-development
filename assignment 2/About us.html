They want to empower young people to actively participate in building more peaceful communities where diversity is celebrated and every single person is valued for who they are.

They also believe in every young person and their ability to fulfill their potential if the necessary supports are put in place. Young people are the change makers in our society, therefore by investing our time and efforts in working with younger generations we are contributing to building more inclusive societies.

They have a non-formal Eurobug Youth Group based in Kilkenny that also includes members from other cities in Ireland, such as Dublin and Waterford.
